TAB UP! — PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 1, 2026
Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Thank you for using Tab Up! Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is stored, and what we do — and do not do — with your data.

⸻

The Short Version

Tab Up stores your information locally on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, sell data, display advertisements, or require user accounts. Your information stays on your iPhone unless you intentionally choose to share it using Apple’s built-in sharing features.

⸻

Information the App Uses

Tab Up may store the following information locally on your device:

* Saved checks, including items, prices, tax, tip, and assigned guests
* Guest information you enter, including names, optional phone numbers, and “Regulars” status
* App preferences, such as default tax/tip percentages and theme selection (Light, Dark, or System)
* Receipt photos that you choose to add or share during a check session

Checks, guest information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple’s local storage systems.

Receipt photos are only temporarily held in memory during use unless you explicitly save or share them through iOS.

We do not upload, transmit, or store your information on external servers.

⸻

Permissions the App Requests

Tab Up may request access to certain iPhone features only when you choose to use related functionality.

Tab Up uses standard Apple iOS frameworks for camera, photo library, and contact access.

You may grant or deny these permissions at any time through your iPhone Settings.

Contacts

Used only when you choose “Choose from Contacts” while adding a guest.

Tab Up accesses only the contact information you select in order to help fill in guest details such as name and phone number.

Tab Up does not:

* read your entire contacts list,
* store your contacts,
* upload contact information,
* or share contact data with third parties.

⸻

Photo Library

Used only when you choose “Choose from Library” while adding a receipt photo.

Tab Up accesses only the photo you select for display during your check session.

The app does not:

* browse your photo library,
* copy unrelated photos,
* or upload images anywhere.

⸻

Camera

Used only when you choose “Take a Photo” while adding a receipt image.

Photos captured through the app remain on your device unless you intentionally share them using iOS sharing features.

⸻

What We Do NOT Do

To be clear, Tab Up does not:

* collect analytics or usage tracking data
* track users across apps or websites
* use third-party advertising networks
* sell, rent, or share personal information
* require user accounts, logins, or passwords
* sync your information to cloud services
* access your contacts, photos, or camera without your direct action

⸻

Data Sharing

We do not share your information because we do not possess your information — your data remains on your device.

If you choose to share a receipt photo or check information through Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or another application, that sharing action is handled by Apple iOS and the app you choose to share with.

Any information shared through third-party apps is governed by the privacy policies of those services.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

Tab Up is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because data remains locally on the device and is not transmitted to us, we do not maintain personal data for any users.

⸻

Your Control and Choices

Because your information stays on your device, you remain in control of your data.

Delete App Data

Deleting the Tab Up app from your iPhone removes locally stored app data, including saved checks, guests, and preferences.

Delete Individual Information

You may remove individual saved checks or guest entries within the app using the available delete options.

Revoke Permissions

You may revoke access to Contacts, Photos, or Camera at any time by going to:

iPhone Settings → Tab Up
